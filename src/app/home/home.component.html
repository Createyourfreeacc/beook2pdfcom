Portable PDFs from your beook library

PDFs you can keep. Read anywhere.

beook can already be used offline — the real advantage of exporting to PDF is independence. A PDF isn’t tied to a vendor licence, and it can be moved to devices that beook doesn’t support. You keep control of your own study materials.
